Geometry Whiz

Open in console and type in commands, such as

  • square (length);
  • cube (length);
  • round (number);
  • areaOfTriangles (base, height);
  • areaOfTriangularPrism (base, height, depth);
  • areaOfRectangle (length, height);
  • cubicVolume (length, height, depth);
  • areaOfCircle (radius);
  • circumferenceOfCircle (radius);
  • volumeOfSphere (radius);
  • surfaceAreaOfSphere (radius);
  • volumeOfCylinder (radius, length);
  • surfaceAreaOfCylinder (radius, length);
  • volumeOfCone (radius, height);
  • surfaceAreaOfCone (radius, slantHeight);
  • lengthOfLine (x1, x2, y1, y2);
  • midpoint (x1, x2, y1, y2);
  • hypotenuse (a, b);
